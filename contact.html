About Us

The Salinas Family

Salinas Ranches LLC is a family-owned cattle operation run by Edgar Salinas and Nina Salinas in Roma, Texas. Located in Starr County along the South Texas border, the ranch sits on open rangeland that has sustained livestock for generations.

The Salinas family raises Hereford cattle — a breed prized for its hardiness in hot climates, calm temperament, and consistent beef quality. The operation focuses on raising healthy, well-cared-for livestock and selling directly to other ranches and livestock buyers.

This isn't a hobby — it's a way of life. Every animal on the ranch gets daily attention, quality feed, and the open space to grow right.
