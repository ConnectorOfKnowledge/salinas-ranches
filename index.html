Our Story

A Family Tradition on South Texas Rangeland

Salinas Ranches LLC is owned and operated by Edgar Salinas and Nina Salinas out of Roma, Texas, in the heart of Starr County. What started as a family passion for working the land and raising cattle has grown into a dedicated livestock operation.

We specialize in Hereford cattle — a breed known for its hardiness, docile temperament, and excellent beef quality. Every animal on our ranch is raised with hands-on care and proper nutrition on South Texas rangeland.
