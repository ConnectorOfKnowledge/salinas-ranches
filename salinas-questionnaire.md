# Website Questionnaire — Salinas Ranches LLC

Hey Edgar and Nina,

Thanks for trusting us with your website. To make sure we build something that truly represents Salinas Ranches, we need to gather some information from y'all. No rush — just work through these questions when you get a chance. If something doesn't apply, skip it. If you're not sure about something, just say so and we'll figure it out together.

---

## 1. Business Basics

- What is the full legal name of your business? (We have **Salinas Ranches LLC** — is that correct?)
- What year was the ranch established?
- What's the full physical address of the ranch in Roma, TX?
- How many acres are you running?
- Roughly how many head of cattle do you have right now?
- Is there anything else you operate besides cattle? (Hay sales, leasing land, etc.)

---

## 2. What You Do

We want to make sure we describe your operation accurately on the site.

- Are you primarily breeding, raising, selling — or all three?
- What do you sell? (Bulls, heifers, cow-calf pairs, bred cows, feeder calves — check all that apply.)
- Are your Herefords registered (with the American Hereford Association) or commercial, or both?
- Do you run any other breeds or crosses, or is it strictly Hereford?
- Do you have a breeding program? If so, what are you selecting for? (Calving ease, growth, temperament, etc.)
- Do you do any embryo transfer or AI (artificial insemination) work?
- Do you sell year-round, or is it seasonal?
- Do you have any sale events, production sales, or do you mostly sell private treaty?
- How do you price your cattle? (Per head, per pound, negotiated, auction price?)
- What's your calving season? (Spring, fall, year-round?)
- What are your typical weaning dates?
- When is your main selling window during the year?

---

## 3. Herd Health and Management

Buyers want to know that the cattle they're getting are healthy and well-managed. This section helps us show that on the site.

- What's your vaccination program? (What vaccines do you give, and at what stages?)
- Do you have a regular deworming schedule?
- Do you work with a veterinarian? If so, would you want to mention that on the site?
- Are your cattle tested for any diseases? (BVD, trich, brucellosis, etc.)
- Do you keep health records on individual animals?
- Are your calves weaned and preconditioned before sale, or sold at the cow's side?

---

## 4. Feeding and Land

This helps paint the picture of how your cattle are raised.

- What do your cattle eat? (Native pasture, improved grasses, hay, supplemental feed, cubes, etc.)
- Do you raise your own hay or buy it?
- What kind of pasture or rangeland do you run? (Native brush, coastal, buffelgrass, etc.)
- What's your water situation? (Wells, stock tanks, river access, etc.)
- Do you have working pens, a loading chute, or other facilities you're proud of?
- Do you practice any kind of rotational grazing or land management?

---

## 5. Sales and Delivery

- Do you deliver cattle to the buyer, or does the buyer pick them up?
- If you deliver, how far are you willing to haul?
- Do you work with any livestock haulers?
- Do you sell through any auction barns? If so, which ones?
- Do you provide any kind of guarantee or return policy on your animals?
- Do you have references or testimonials from past buyers who'd be willing to be quoted on the site?

---

## 6. Shows, Competitions, and Industry

- Have you or your cattle ever competed in any livestock shows or competitions?
- Any ribbons, banners, or trophies you'd want featured on the site?
- Are you involved in any 4-H or FFA programs?
- Any involvement with the American Hereford Association or other breed registries?
- Are there any industry events, expos, or sales you regularly attend?

---

## 7. Your Customers

Since this is a B2B operation, we want to speak directly to the people who buy from you.

- Who are your typical buyers? (Other ranches, feedlots, auction houses, order buyers, etc.)
- Where are most of your customers located? (South Texas, statewide, out of state?)
- How do buyers usually find you right now? (Word of mouth, auctions, social media?)
- What do your customers care about most when they buy from you? (Genetics, price, consistency, your reputation?)
- Do you have any long-term buyer relationships you'd like to mention or highlight on the site?

---

## 8. What Makes Salinas Ranches Different

This is the stuff that sets you apart. Don't be modest — brag a little.

- How long has the Salinas family been ranching? How many generations?
- Is there a family story or history behind the ranch you'd want people to know?
- What's your ranching philosophy? (How you raise your cattle, how you treat the land, etc.)
- Are you members of any breed associations or industry organizations?
- Any awards, recognitions, or certifications?
- Do you have a registered brand? If so, what does it look like?
- Is there anything you do differently from other Hereford operations in your area?

---

## 9. Contact Info for the Website

What do you want people to see when they visit the site and want to reach out?

- What phone number should be listed? (One number or separate ones for Edgar and Nina?)
- What email address should we use?
- Do you want the full physical address on the site, or just "Roma, TX"?
- Do you have any social media accounts? (Facebook, Instagram, etc.) If so, what are the links?
- Do you prefer people call, text, email, or use a contact form on the site?
- Are there specific hours you'd like listed, or is it more of a "call anytime" situation?

---

## 10. Photos and Media

Good photos make a huge difference. Here's what we need to know.

- Do you have a logo? If so, can you send us the file? (Preferably a high-resolution PNG or vector file.)
- Do you have a brand mark (the actual cattle brand)? We might want to use that as a design element.
- Do you have any professional photos of the ranch, your cattle, or your family? If so, please send us everything you've got — we'll pick the best ones.
- Do you have any drone footage or aerial photos of the property?
- Would you be open to us arranging a photo or video shoot if needed?
- Are there any photos you definitely want on the site? (A favorite bull, a family photo, the front gate, etc.)

---

## 11. Website Specifics

This helps us understand what you're picturing.

- Have you seen any other ranch websites you like? (Send us links if you have them — it helps us understand your taste.)
- Are there any websites you've seen that you did NOT like? (Just as helpful.)
- Is there anything specific you want on the site? For example:
  - A page listing cattle currently for sale?
  - A history/about page?
  - A photo gallery?
  - A contact form?
  - Directions or a map to the ranch?
- Is there anything you definitely do NOT want on the site? (Pricing, certain photos, etc.)
- Do you plan to update the site yourself (adding new cattle for sale, etc.), or would you rather have us handle updates?
- Do you already own a domain name (like salinasranches.com)? If not, do you have a preference for one?

---

## 12. Timeline and Budget

Just so we can plan things out and make sure we're on the same page.

- Is there a specific date or event you'd like the site ready for? (A sale, a show, tax season, etc.)
- What's your general budget range for the website? (This helps us figure out the right scope — no wrong answer here.)
- Are you interested in ongoing maintenance after the site is live, or is this more of a one-time build?
- Anything else on your mind that we didn't ask about?

---

That's it! Like I said, no rush. Just fill in what you can and send it back whenever you're ready. If it's easier to hop on a call and go through these together, we're happy to do that too.

Looking forward to building something great for Salinas Ranches.

Talk soon!
